Baby Belle

Belle was found on the streets of East Lansing by my sister in 2011, and at the time she was mostly fur.

She has evolved into a cheese loving machine (especially Babybel cheese wheels),
and has been struggling on a weight loss journey for the last 5 years after making up for her previous starvation.
Belle dislikes dressing up for Halloween but begrudgingly accepted being Taco Belle (pictured right) for her mom.

Belle is also known for stealing pizza out of people's mouths, her love of sushi (without fish), napping in the sun and grazing grass in her backyard.
